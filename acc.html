e-Business and e-Government Trends Report

Project Brief: The final project of a summer internship at Accenture Consulting. Read relevant reports and case studies in order to compile an all encompassing report that included new trends that would allow both the public and private sector to come up with new e-Business and e-Governemnt strategies. Used as a foundation for new consulting projects at Accenture.

First conducted a literature review on reports and surverys that analyzed the current use of e-Government and e-Business strategies and their success globally, focusing primarily on the EU and Greece. Then, I analyzed relevant technological trends and came up with a model that outlines how governments and businesses should proceed on moving from analog to digital and avoid becoming outdated.

My role in the project:

  • In charge of doing the research for the project
  • Compiled and wrote the report
  • Presented the final report at the Wellesley Tanner Conference 2014 and to senior employees at Accenture
