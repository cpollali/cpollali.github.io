Harvard Business School
Time Management Project

Project Brief: The HCI Lab at Wellesley College collaborated with a Harvard Business School professor to create an interactive visualization of a CEO's time managemenet before a merger and acquisition for a case study in the business school. Evolved into a project that uses a Google Extension to extract any user's data to create an interactive visualization. The goal of this project is to allow users to better understand how they distribute their time in an attempt for better future time management.

Figure 1. Interactive Visualization for Time Management

A user can download their google calendar data to create the above visualization. They can then interact with the data by changing which categories are shown, view a specific week, or showing the raw data.

My role in the project:

  • Parsing the data extracted from the Google extension to convert it to the correct format used by the visualization
  • Coding (JavaScript, HTML, CSS) the front end of the interactive visualization
