About me

My name is Christina Pollalis and I graduated from Wellesley College in 2016, where I studied Political Science and Computer Science. I am currently working in the Wellesley Human-Computer Interaction Lab doing user experience research, project management, and software development.


I create and develop novel user interfaces that faciliate understanding and exploration of information by defining project vision and strategy, leading weekly meetings, and assigning tasks.


In my projects, I collaborate with New York University, the Harvard Personal Genome Project, the Teach Museum of Innovation in San Jose, and the Davis Museum at Wellesley College on exciting projects that use augmented reality, virtual reality, and MultiTaction technologies. I have also authored 5 publications at top-tier ACM conferences.
